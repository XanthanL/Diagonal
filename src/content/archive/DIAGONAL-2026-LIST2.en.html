List Announcement | The Salt of Life 2.0: Zigong Salt Culture Research & Creation Residency

Location: ZG / Zigong

Following a rigorous review process and in-depth local considerations, the selected list of creators for The Salt of Life 2.0 — Zigong Salt Culture Research & Creation Residency is now officially announced. From a large pool of applicants, we have selected practitioners concerned with industrial remnants, community narratives, and place-based cultural practice to embark together on the second phase of in-situ creation.

Creative Team

In The Salt of Life 2.0, we expect creators to delve further into the social fabric of the Huojingtuo area, combining academic research on salt culture with contemporary art methodologies to produce works with greater place-based resonance.

Future Plans

This residency will run until June 2026. During the residency, selected creators will complete works based on place-based research in Zigong, with outcomes presented through exhibitions, workshops, site-specific interactions, and other formats.

Notice for Selected Creators

Please keep a close eye on the project communication group. We will reach out one-on-one regarding follow-up residency details.
