Volunteers and Interns | 志愿者与实习生招募

Roles you can take on during the exhibition:

1. Media and Event Operations: Responsible for editing and publishing WeChat Official Account content from exhibition preparation through closing, as well as planning and executing related events.

2. Pre-opening and Opening Operations Execution: Responsible for the offline exhibition space execution leading up to the opening, working with the curatorial team to complete the exhibition tasks.

3. Exhibition-period Operations: Responsible for reception at the exhibition space, artwork guarding, and assisting with merchandise sales.

You may choose one or more of the above roles. Other position needs will be posted as they arise, and relevant certificates can be issued to all interns.

Feedback and Benefits for Participants | 参与人员可获得的回馈

1. Exhibition-related product gift package

2. Accommodation can be arranged during the volunteer and internship period (at the residency dormitory)

3. Priority recommendation for future Diagonal project collaborations, and entry into a long-term talent pool for art management

Specific internship schedules can be discussed later.
