Schedule & List Announcement: The Salt of Life 1.0 — Zigong Residency

Location: ZG / Zigong

After careful selection, we are excited to announce the list of selected creators and the detailed schedule for The Salt of Life 1.0 residency. The salt culture remnants of Huojingtuo, Zigong, await your discovery and reconstruction.

Schedule

This residency will construct a multi-dimensional knowledge and experience map through immersive investigation, community collaboration, and place-based creation.

Date Activity
13 March Arrival, site familiarisation, and local briefing.
14–17 March Field investigation of ancient salt roads and data collection.
18–25 March In-depth community research and creative experiments.
From 26 March Presentation of residency outcomes, exchange, and exhibition installation.

We look forward to meeting you in Zigong and writing the memory of salt together.

Note

All selected creators must arrive at the designated residency on time.
