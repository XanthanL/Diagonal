Exhibition Executive | 展览执行

Vivienne Peng | 彭玮雯

Intermedia artist

Researcher in socially engaged art and contemporary art

Co-initiator of "Diagonal"

University of Plymouth, UK
PhD candidate (Practice-based Art Research)
Jointly trained PhD candidate at Soochow University
Master of Experimental Art, Sichuan Fine Arts Institute

Her doctoral research, "Co-constructing Material Memory: A Methodology of Socially Engaged Art Based on Industrial Heritage in China's 'Post-Danwei' Communities", conducts ongoing cross-regional art practice in the Ziliujing Salt Factory community of Zigong, Sichuan and the Junde Coal Mine community of Hegang, Heilongjiang, transforming material memory, bodily experience, and industrial heritage into socially engaged art sites. Master's research topic: "Reconstruction of Memory: On the Transformation and Image Production of the Zigong Salt Factory". Her works and research have been exhibited and published in the UK, Singapore, France, and multiple locations in China, and she has presented at the China Media Materiality Forum, Exclamation! International Art Conference, UoP FoAHB Doctoral Conference, Tropical Lab, and the Ruthin International Live Art Festival.
