Open Call | The Salt of Life 1.0: Zigong Salt Culture Research & Creation Residency

Location: ZG / Zigong

Why “The Salt of Life”?

Salt is mineral, salt is body, salt is industry, salt is time. Salt is everything. Through this residency, we hope salt will no longer be merely a historical resource. We hope you, coming to Zigong, will find your own answer.

The Salt of Life residency series will continue until June 2026. This round is the first phase (1.0), expected to be completed in February–March.

Who Can Apply

Artists, curators, researchers, architects / designers, scholars in anthropology / sociology and other cultural studies, as well as cross-disciplinary practitioners concerned with industrial heritage and community transformation.

Residency Time and Location

Dates: 13–15 March 2026 (flexible, subject to specific circumstances).
Location: Zigong City, Sichuan Province.

Shared Residency Spaces

Huojingtuo Creators’ Dormitory (co-living space)

Lǎo Studio (material experiment and creation studio)

SaltArt Space (public display and exchange space)

Partner art space: DreamJIO (located in Gongjing Old Street)

Residency Schedule

Phase | Core Content Description
Day 1-4: Urban Investigation Project introduction and overview of Zigong salt culture history. Walking investigations: Salt Industry History Museum, Shenhai Well, old salt works, 1957 Creative Park, thousand-year salt road, Ziliujing Old Street, Gongjing Old Street, etc.
Day 5-6: Community Research Place-based research, resident interviews, video and sound collection. Focusing creative topics, material experiments, sketches.
Day 7-15: Place-based Creation Creation in shared studio (media open), internal discussion and adjustment, exhibition installation.
Day 15-20: Exhibition Opening Workshops, dialogues and sharing sessions, online and offline activities.

Support & Provision

Diagonal provides: free accommodation, studio and exhibition space, community resource connection, exhibition and event organisation, public platform presentation and promotion.
