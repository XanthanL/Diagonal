Co-living & Co-empathy: The Salt of Life 1.0 — Live Interaction Action Call at the Residency Outcomes Exhibition

Location: ZG / Zigong

At the closing stage of The Salt of Life 1.0 residency, we invite everyone to Huojingtuo, Zigong, to participate in the live interaction action at the residency outcomes exhibition. This is not merely an exhibition, but a collective practice of co-living, co-empathy, and place-based experience.

Exhibition Context

The history of Zigong’s salt industry is not only a record of production, but also a history of how people and communities collaborated, survived, and emotionally connected under extreme conditions. “Co-living” refers not only to physical cohabitation, but to the emotional resonance achieved through art practice amid the ruins and vitality of industrial heritage.

Call for On-site Participants

We are recruiting partners to engage in interactive actions with the creators at the exhibition. This includes, but is not limited to: participatory narrative guidance, handling and interacting with salt materials, and feedback collection for community oral history.

Participants will have the opportunity to communicate directly with resident artists and understand how they “rewrite history through salt” on the land of Huojingtuo.

Format

No specific art background is required — only curiosity about place-based space, material memory, and interpersonal connection. We will carry out the action in small-scale workshops within the exhibition hall.

How to Participate

Please register at the exhibition entrance, or join the on-site interaction guide group directly during opening hours.
