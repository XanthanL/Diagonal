Field of Salt Memory: A4 International Residency Art Center Chengdu Art Week Sharing Session

Location: CD / Chengdu

In April 2026, as an important component of Chengdu Art Week, Diagonal was invited to a sharing session hosted by the A4 International Residency Art Center. Under the theme “Field of Salt Memory,” the event explored material research and place-based dialogue in residency creation.

Theme: Reshaping the Memory of Matter and Space

Within the context of Chengdu Art Week, Diagonal reviewed its long-term cross-regional practice from northeast to southwest, focusing on the residency journey in Huojingtuo, Zigong. Using “salt” as an entry point, we discussed how place-based culture can catalyse contemporary art creation.

The sharing session presented not only visual archives and physical models from the residency, but also invited art educators and cultural practitioners into dialogue, examining the project’s sociological significance from a broader cultural ecology perspective.

We further analysed the complexities encountered during residency creation: how to negotiate the tension between the heavy history of industrial remnants and individual creative expression.

About This Sharing Session

As part of the Chengdu Art Week series, this sharing session was not only a phase summary of the project, but also a public test of place-based cultural narrative.
